Sports Life

I started playing sports at a very young age, i did everything from hockey, tennis and swimming.I played hockey in Harlequins Hockey Club for 13 years and played school hockey for 6 years. In this period we would 3 Munster cups and 4 League cups in total.While playing hockey i also completed my Irish Water Safety course and am now a qualified Lifequard. To qualify i completed a four year course which included a year of endurance training and final year exam.I also competed in many swimming galas over the years and have achieved many 1st and 2nd place trophies.
